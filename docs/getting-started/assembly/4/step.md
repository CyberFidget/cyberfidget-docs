## 4

<p class="muted">Gather: (1) Case Front, (2) Buttons, (3) Reference Layout</p>

<p class="muted">1 - Using the Reference Layout (unless you want to reprogram the button layout), insert the buttons into the Case Front. The buttons and case have a reasonably close fit to reduce rattling, and will require0 inserting them perpendicularly (4). There should be very little, if at all, force involved.</p>

<p class="muted">ℹ️ (5) You can you use your fingers to hold the buttons in while you flip the case over to see if it matches the layout you want.<br>ℹ️ (6) For extra fun after assembly, you can fill the grooves with your choice of paint to add extra contrast. Acrylic paint pens work reasonably well and are very affordable.</p>
