# Beta Access

Firmware editing, compiling, and installing from the browser are in closed beta. If you are signed in, you can request access from the invitation card on one of those surfaces or from **Request beta access** in the account menu.

---

## Send a request

1. Sign in to your account.
2. Open an invitation card for a beta feature, or choose **Request beta access** from the account menu.
3. Add an optional note if there is context you want to share.
4. Submit the request.

We review these by hand.

---

## Understand your request status

Your account shows the current state honestly:

- **Pending** means your request is waiting for review.
- **Approved** means the beta features are unlocked for your account.
- **Not yet** includes the date when you can ask again. The wait is 14 days.

You do not need to take an extra unlock step after approval. The beta features simply become available.
