# Accessories

Cyber Fidget is designed for customization. Swap parts, change colors, and make it yours.

---

## Color gels

Interchangeable color gels sit between the LEDs and the aluminum chassis, changing how the RGBW light looks from the outside.

Available colors include:

- **Red** — warm, saturated glow
- **Teal** — cool accent tone
- **Blue** — cool accent tone
- More colors planned

Gels are easy to swap — just remove the backplate screws and slide them in or out.

---

## Backplates

Multiple machined aluminum backplate designs are available. The backplate attaches with the main case screws and can be swapped.

---

## 3D printing

Enclosure models are available for 3D printing, so you can design and print your own backplates, cases, or modifications.

!!! tip "Design your own"
    The aluminum chassis dimensions are documented in the [Hardware Specs](../hardware/specs.md). Use these as reference when designing custom parts. STL files are also planned to be made available.
